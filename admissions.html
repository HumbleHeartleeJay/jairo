Admissions

Learn the admissions and enrollment requirements for every level. Review the checklist for your student, then follow the step-by-step enrollment procedure below.

Requirements Checklist

What to prepare, by level

Nursery – Kindergarten

  • PSA Birth Certificate (1 photocopy)

Grade 1

  • ECCD Checklist (original copy)
  • PSA Birth Certificate (1 photocopy)

Grade 2 – Grade 10

  • Report Card (Form 138-A)
  • PSA Birth Certificate (1 photocopy)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character

Grade 11 – Grade 12

  • Report Card (F-138A)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • Certificate of Completion in Junior High
  • ESC Certificate (private school completer)

Freshmen (College)

  • 4 pcs 1x1 ID picture
  • Drug test result
  • Report Card (F-138)
  • Medical certificate
  • PSA Birth Certificate
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • Marriage contract (if married)

Transferee (College)

  • TOR (evaluative copy)
  • Honorable dismissal
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • PSA Birth Certificate
  • Medical certificate
  • Drug test result
  • 4 pcs 1x1 ID picture
  • Marriage contract (if married)

Diploma Program

  • Report Card (F-138) — High School / Senior High
  • Transcript of Records (College)
  • PSA Birth Certificate (1 photocopy)
  • Indigent Certificate (from Barangay)
  • Proof of Residency (DSWD)
  • VAX Card (photocopy)
Enrollment Procedures

Step-by-step, by student type

Tap a category to expand its enrollment steps.

  1. See the Guidance-in-charge for the entrance examination / interview.
  2. Secure the Eligibility Form from the Principal's Office.
  3. Present the PSA Birth Certificate and Eligibility Form to the Registrar's Office; secure and accomplish the Registration Form.
  4. Proceed to the Finance Office for tuition levelling.
  5. Pay the enrollment fee at the cashier.
  6. Go back to the Registrar's Office.
